# AI & Machine Learning Weekly Update — May 6, 2026

## Highlights

- **AMI Labs raises $1.03B in record-breaking seed round**: Yann LeCun's Paris-based startup building "world models" secures the largest seed round in European history at a $3.5 billion valuation, signaling a major bet on alternatives to traditional LLM architectures.
- **Google DeepMind upgrades Gemini 3 Deep Think**: The specialized reasoning mode receives a major upgrade focused on practical scientific applications, now available to Ultra subscribers and opening API access to researchers.
- **Sakana AI introduces KAME speech architecture**: A novel tandem speech-to-speech system that enables real-time LLM knowledge injection with near-zero latency, shifting the paradigm from "think then speak" to "speak while thinking."

## Detailed Updates

### AMI Labs Secures Record $1.03B Seed Funding

Advanced Machine Intelligence (AMI) Labs, founded by Turing Award winner Yann LeCun, raised $1.03 billion in seed funding at a $3.5 billion valuation — the largest seed round in European history. The Paris-based startup is developing "world models," an alternative AI architecture to large language models that trains on real-world data to embody virtual, interactive, and 3D environments. This represents a significant philosophical divergence from the autoregressive text-generation paradigm that has dominated recent AI development.

### Google Gemini 3 Deep Think Major Upgrade

Google released a significant upgrade to Gemini 3 Deep Think, their specialized reasoning mode designed for complex scientific and mathematical problem-solving. The updated model is now live in the Gemini app for Ultra subscribers, with early API access opening for researchers, engineers, and enterprises. Deep Think is positioned as Google's answer to extended reasoning capabilities, competing directly with OpenAI's o-series reasoning models.

### Sakana AI KAME: Real-Time Knowledge Injection for Speech

Tokyo-based Sakana AI introduced KAME, a tandem speech-to-speech architecture that injects LLM knowledge in real-time. The system uses a four-stream architecture extending Moshi's design with an oracle stream for knowledge injection, allowing voice AI to respond immediately while an LLM reasons in parallel. Notably, the system is backend-agnostic, supporting GPT-4.1, Claude Opus, or Gemini Flash without retraining.

### Cloudflare Builds High-Performance LLM Infrastructure

Cloudflare announced new infrastructure designed to run large AI language models across its global network. The system separates model input processing and output generation onto different optimized systems and uses a custom inference engine for more efficient GPU management, addressing the challenge of serving increasingly large models at scale.

### Novo Nordisk Partners with OpenAI

Novo Nordisk announced a strategic partnership with OpenAI to integrate AI across its entire business — from drug discovery and clinical trials to manufacturing — with full deployment planned by end of 2026. The partnership aims to accelerate identification of new obesity and diabetes treatments and represents one of the largest pharma-AI integrations to date.

### Mollifier Layers for Inverse PDEs

Researchers at the University of Pennsylvania's School of Engineering introduced "Mollifier Layers," a novel technique for solving inverse partial differential equations with applications in genomics, materials science, and climate modeling. The findings are set to be presented at NeurIPS 2026.

### Anthropic Launches Financial AI Agents

Anthropic released ten preconfigured AI agents designed specifically for the financial sector, automating typical tasks performed by investment banks, asset managers, and insurers. This marks a strategic push into vertical enterprise applications.

### OpenAI Raises $10B from Wall Street Consortium

OpenAI received $10 billion from a 19-firm Wall Street consortium, while Anthropic is closing a $1.5 billion funding round from Blackstone, Goldman Sachs, and Hellman & Friedman — underscoring continued massive capital flows into frontier AI development.
