# AI for CAD Weekly Update — April 29, 2026

## Highlights

- **Autodesk Neural CAD Advances**: Autodesk's AI foundation model, evolving from Project Bernini, is approaching a milestone in generating fully editable CAD geometry from text prompts, though the gap between marketing and working reality remains wide.
- **SOLIDWORKS 2026 Embeds AI Companions**: Dassault Systèmes introduced LEO and MARIE, two AI virtual companions built into the 3DEXPERIENCE platform for interactive design collaboration, detailing, and assembly modeling.
- **AI Design Review Delivers ROI Now**: Industry analysis shows that while generative text-to-CAD remains aspirational, AI-powered design review and verification tools are already delivering measurable returns in production environments.

## Detailed Updates

### Autodesk Neural CAD Foundation Model
Autodesk continues development of Neural CAD, an AI foundation model capable of generating fully editable CAD geometry from a single text prompt. The system builds on Autodesk's earlier Project Bernini research and is trained on proprietary manufacturing-focused datasets. While demonstrations show promise, industry analysts note that text-to-CAD remains the area where the gap between marketing claims and practical results is widest in the CAD AI space.

### SOLIDWORKS 2026 AI Virtual Companions
Dassault Systèmes' SOLIDWORKS 2026 release introduces LEO and MARIE, two AI-powered virtual companions accessible through the 3DEXPERIENCE platform. These companions are designed for interactive collaboration during design sessions — assisting with drawing creation, detailing, assembly modeling, and design exploration. The integration marks a significant step in normalizing generative AI within everyday mechanical design workflows.

### Siemens NX Copilot Expansion
Siemens continues expanding its NX CAD Copilot, built in partnership with Microsoft, enabling conversational interaction within the Designcenter NX environment. Users can ask questions about their designs, explore materials choices, and try out design options interactively through natural language. The copilot is now integrated across more NX workflows including simulation setup and manufacturing planning.

### AI Design Review vs. Generative Design
A detailed industry analysis from CoLab Software highlights a divergence in AI CAD maturity: AI-powered design review tools are delivering immediate ROI by automating quality checks, catching errors early, and streamlining approval workflows. In contrast, generative design — where AI creates geometry from scratch — is still catching up and not yet reliable enough for production use in most engineering contexts.

### Leo AI and Text-to-CAD Progress
Leo AI and other startups continue pushing text-to-CAD capabilities, with the technology best suited currently for early-stage concept exploration and initial geometry generation rather than production-ready output. The consensus among engineers is that AI-generated models still require significant manual refinement before they meet manufacturing tolerances.

### Three AI Features Coming to Every CAD Program
Engineering.com identified three AI capabilities that are now appearing across all major CAD platforms in 2026: intelligent constraint suggestion (AI proposes geometric relationships), automated detailing (AI generates drawings from 3D models with standards-compliant annotations), and design-for-manufacturing feedback (real-time DFM analysis during modeling). These represent the practical, near-term AI capabilities that are changing daily workflows.
