### Industry Landscape: AI Features Now Standard Across CAD

A notable industry pattern has solidified: AI features are now embedded in every major CAD program. Key developments that matured into production use this quarter include Autodesk's Neural CAD foundation model generating fully editable parametric geometry from text prompts, Autodesk Fusion's Automated Drawings using AI to classify fasteners and generate assembly documentation, SOLIDWORKS 2026's AI companions LEO and MARIE for interactive design collaboration, and Siemens Solid Edge's AI feature generating up to 80% of drawing views automatically. The consensus among practitioners is that the most successful production workflows remain hybrid: AI generates base meshes, textures, and documentation drafts, with manual refinement for production quality.

Sources: [Engineering.com](https://www.engineering.com/3-ai-features-coming-to-every-cad-program-in-2026/) · [Leo AI](https://www.getleo.ai/blog/can-ai-generate-cad-models) · [SOLIDWORKS](https://www.solidworks.com/solution/how-ai-is-augmenting-cad-tools-better-product-design)

### Text-to-3D Tools Continue Maturing

The broader AI 3D modeling ecosystem continues to evolve, with tools like Tripo AI (H3.1 and P1.0 models), Meshy AI, and Autodesk's Wonder 3D in Flow Studio all seeing active development. Production adoption remains focused on generating base assets for manual refinement rather than end-to-end automated design.

Sources: [Autodesk Flow Studio](https://adsknews.autodesk.com/en/news/autodesk-flow-studio-launches-wonder-3d-gen-ai-model-to-help-creators-make-3d-characters-and-objects-faster-and-easier/)
