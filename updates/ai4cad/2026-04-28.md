# AI for CAD Weekly Update — April 28, 2026

## Highlights

- **Autodesk Neural CAD Generates Editable Geometry from Text**: A foundation model that creates fully parametric, editable CAD models from text prompts, moving beyond conceptual generation to production-ready geometry.
- **SOLIDWORKS 2026 Embeds Generative AI Throughout**: Two new AI companions, LEO and MARIE, bring interactive collaboration to everyday mechanical design workflows.
- **Siemens Solid Edge AI Automates 80% of Drawing Views**: AI features now generate the majority of technical drawing views with minimal user input.

## Detailed Updates

### Autodesk Neural CAD Foundation Model

Autodesk's Neural CAD represents a significant leap in AI-assisted design — a foundation model capable of generating fully editable CAD geometry from a single text prompt. Unlike earlier generative design tools that produce mesh-based suggestions requiring extensive cleanup, Neural CAD delivers parametric, editable models that engineers can immediately refine within the CAD kernel. This moves the industry from AI as a suggestion engine to AI as a first-draft design partner.

### SOLIDWORKS 2026 AI Integration

Dassault Systèmes' SOLIDWORKS 2026 release marks the normalization of generative AI in everyday mechanical design. The software now embeds AI directly into drawing creation, detailing, and assembly modeling workflows. Two new Virtual Companions — LEO and MARIE — provide interactive AI collaboration through the 3DEXPERIENCE platform. LEO assists with design exploration and optimization, while MARIE focuses on manufacturing readiness assessment.

### Siemens Solid Edge AI Drawing Automation

Siemens introduced an AI feature in Solid Edge that generates up to 80% of drawing views with minimal user input, dramatically reducing the time spent on technical documentation. The system analyzes 3D models and intelligently selects appropriate views, cross-sections, and detail views based on engineering conventions and part geometry.

### Autodesk Fusion Automated Drawings

Autodesk Fusion's Automated Drawings feature combines templates and heuristics with AI to create complete assembly drawings. The system uses an AI model to classify standard fasteners and automatically applies appropriate drawing conventions, reducing documentation time from hours to minutes for complex assemblies.

### Wonder 3D in Autodesk Flow Studio

Autodesk's Wonder 3D generative AI model, launched in Flow Studio, enables text-to-3D, image-to-3D, and text-to-image creation for artists and studios. While primarily targeted at media and entertainment workflows, the technology signals the convergence of creative 3D generation with engineering design tools.

### AI 3D Modeling Landscape Maturing

The broader AI 3D modeling ecosystem continues to mature in 2026. Tripo AI's H3.1 and P1.0 models deliver measurable gains in geometric precision and generation speed by modeling geometry directly in 3D space rather than lifting from 2D. Meshy AI remains one of the most well-rounded tools for text-to-3D creation. However, industry consensus remains that the most successful production workflows use a hybrid approach: AI generates base meshes and textures, with manual refinement for production quality.

### CAD AI Features Becoming Standard

A clear industry pattern has emerged: AI features are now present in every major CAD program. Beyond geometry generation, these include AI chatbots trained on software documentation to assist users, intelligent automation of repetitive design tasks, and AI-driven manufacturing feasibility analysis. The shift is from AI as an optional add-on to AI as embedded infrastructure.
