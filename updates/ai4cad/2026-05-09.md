# AI for CAD Weekly Update — May 9, 2026

## Highlights

- **SOLIDWORKS Launches LEO and MARIE Virtual Companions**: Dassault Systèmes announced two AI-powered virtual companions for SOLIDWORKS Design — LEO for mechanical engineering workflows and MARIE for materials science and chemistry — accessible through the 3DEXPERIENCE platform.
- **Siemens Solid Edge AI Generates 80% of Drawing Views Automatically**: Siemens added an AI feature to Solid Edge that automatically generates up to 80% of drawing views with minimal user input, dramatically accelerating the documentation phase of the design process.
- **Text-to-CAD Tools See Rapid Development**: Text-to-CAD capabilities, where engineers describe parts in natural language and AI produces 3D models, have become the most active area of development in the CAD AI space in 2026.

## Detailed Updates

### SOLIDWORKS LEO and MARIE Companions
Dassault Systèmes introduced two new AI virtual companions for SOLIDWORKS Design on the 3DEXPERIENCE platform. LEO specializes in mechanical design, assisting engineers with design workflows, parametric modeling, and engineering calculations. MARIE brings materials science and chemistry expertise, helping with material selection, properties analysis, and scientific research integration. Both are designed for interactive, conversational collaboration during the design process.

### Siemens Solid Edge Automated Drawings
Siemens Solid Edge now includes an AI feature that generates up to 80% of drawing views automatically with minimal user input. This addresses one of the most time-consuming phases of the engineering workflow — translating 3D designs into 2D manufacturing documentation. The feature combines template-based approaches with AI inference to determine optimal view orientations and section cuts.

### Autodesk Fusion Automated Drawings
Autodesk Fusion's Automated Drawings feature uses a combination of templates, heuristics, and AI to create complete sets of engineering drawings for assemblies. The system intelligently selects views, dimensions, and annotations based on the geometry and intent of the 3D model.

### SketchUp AI Render by Trimble
Trimble released SketchUp AI Render, making SketchUp the first major CAD program to integrate generative rendering capabilities. The feature uses AI to quickly create photorealistic renders from 3D models, with text prompts enabling rapid exploration of material, lighting, and environmental variations without traditional rendering setup.

### Text-to-CAD Advances
Text-to-CAD tools represent the frontier of AI-assisted design in 2026. Engineers can now describe parts or assemblies in natural language and receive generated 3D models. While accuracy still requires human verification and refinement, the technology is advancing rapidly with startups like Leo AI, Zoo.dev, and others pushing the boundaries of what's possible from language-to-geometry translation.

### Generative Design Maturation
Generative design workflows have matured significantly, with engineers now able to input constraints including materials, weight, cost, and performance targets, while AI generates hundreds or thousands of optimized design alternatives. The technology is shifting from experimental to production use, particularly in aerospace and automotive applications where weight optimization directly impacts performance.

### 3D AI Moving to Real-Time
The broader 3D AI landscape in 2026 is shifting from static asset generation to interactive, real-time experiences. Key improvements include better text-to-mesh quality, faster processing workflows, stronger real-time rendering integration, and more agent-like design tools that can iterate autonomously within defined constraints.
