# AI for CAD Weekly Update — May 7, 2026

## Highlights

- **SOLIDWORKS Introduces LEO and MARIE Virtual Companions**: Two new AI companions coming to SOLIDWORKS Design in 2026, accessible through the 3DEXPERIENCE platform, focused on interactive collaboration rather than one-off tool use.
- **Zoo Ships Conversational CAD Agent "Zookeeper"**: Zoo Design Studio v1.1 includes Zookeeper, an AI assistant that can create models, inspect projects, understand selections, and perform design reviews conversationally.
- **Autodesk Fusion Gets Natural Language Design**: Autodesk Fusion now supports natural language prompts that translate directly into design actions, lowering the barrier for complex parametric modeling.

## Detailed Updates

### SOLIDWORKS LEO and MARIE Virtual Companions
Dassault Systèmes is introducing two new virtual AI companions for SOLIDWORKS Design in 2026. LEO and MARIE are designed for interactive collaboration through the 3DEXPERIENCE platform, offering capabilities including command prediction, automated generative engineering drawings, creating parametric models from pictures, and generating 3D CAD models with text and voice prompts. The emphasis is on continuous collaboration rather than isolated AI features.

### Zoo's Zookeeper Conversational CAD Agent
Zoo shipped its conversational agent "Zookeeper" with Zoo Design Studio v1.1 in January 2026, and adoption has continued to grow through the spring. Zookeeper extends Zoo's hybrid workflow by introducing AI-assisted modeling: it can create new models, inspect existing projects, understand what is selected, answer questions, and perform design reviews. Zoo also offers its ML CAD Model Generator for text-to-CAD file generation through the platform.

### Autodesk Fusion Natural Language Integration
Autodesk Fusion now supports AI-powered natural language prompts, turning conversational descriptions into real design actions within the parametric modeling environment. This marks a significant step toward making professional CAD tools accessible to users who may not be experts in traditional CAD workflows, while still producing fully editable parametric geometry.

### Spectral Labs Building "ChatGPT for CAD"
Spectral Labs continues development of their SGS-1 model, which allows engineers to input text descriptions, rough 2D sketches, or 3D mesh scans to generate fully editable, parametric 3D CAD files. The approach represents one of the most ambitious text-to-CAD efforts in the industry, though production readiness varies significantly by use case.

### Backflip AI: 3D Scans to Parametric Models
Backflip AI offers a plug-in and stand-alone app that converts data from 3D scanners into fully parametric CAD models. This addresses a longstanding pain point in reverse engineering workflows, where scan-to-CAD conversion has traditionally required extensive manual reconstruction.

### AI Features Now Standard Across CAD Programs
Three AI features are becoming ubiquitous across every major CAD program in 2026: automated drawings that combine templates and heuristics with AI, intelligent command prediction that learns from user behavior, and AI-assisted design review that catches errors before manufacturing. The integration of generative design capabilities means engineers can now input constraints like materials, weight, cost, and performance goals to receive hundreds of optimized design alternatives.

### Reality Check: What Actually Works
While the tooling has advanced significantly, industry observers note that AI-generated CAD output quality and usability still vary enormously depending on the tool and use case. Topology optimization and generative design often produce geometry requiring significant post-processing, and text-to-CAD tools work best for simpler components rather than complex assemblies.
