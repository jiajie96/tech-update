# AI for CAD Weekly Update — May 14, 2026

## Highlights

- **Autodesk Neural CAD Generates Editable Geometry from Text**: Autodesk's Neural CAD foundation model can now produce fully parametric, editable CAD models from a single text prompt, moving beyond earlier generative tools that required manual reinterpretation of outputs.
- **SOLIDWORKS 2026 Embeds Generative AI in Core Workflows**: Dassault Systemes' SOLIDWORKS 2026 release normalizes generative AI within everyday mechanical design by embedding it directly into drawing creation, detailing, and assembly modeling.
- **Siemens Solid Edge Automates Drawing View Generation**: Siemens added an AI feature to Solid Edge that generates up to 80% of drawing views with minimal user input, significantly reducing documentation overhead.

## Detailed Updates

### Autodesk Neural CAD Foundation Model
Autodesk's Neural CAD represents a major leap in AI-powered design, capable of generating fully editable CAD geometry from text prompts. Unlike earlier generative design tools that produced optimization outputs requiring significant reinterpretation, Neural CAD delivers parametric, editable models that engineers can immediately refine. Demonstrations showed how a simple prompt could generate a baseline air fryer design with proper feature trees and dimension constraints intact.

### SOLIDWORKS 2026 AI Integration
Dassault Systemes' SOLIDWORKS 2026 marks a significant step in normalizing generative AI within everyday mechanical design workflows. The release embeds AI directly into drawing creation, detailing, and assembly modeling, rather than treating it as a separate optimization step. Two new Virtual Companions, LEO and MARIE, enable interactive collaboration through the 3DEXPERIENCE platform, while the AURA AI assistant provides context-aware assistance across the design process.

### Siemens Solid Edge AI-Powered Drawing Views
Siemens added an AI feature to Solid Edge that can generate up to 80% of standard drawing views with minimal user input. The system analyzes 3D model geometry and automatically suggests appropriate orthographic, section, and detail views, dramatically reducing the time engineers spend on documentation while maintaining compliance with drafting standards.

### NVIDIA-Dassault Industrial AI Architecture
NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang and Dassault Systemes CEO Pascal Daloz announced a partnership to build a shared industrial AI architecture, merging virtual twins with physics-based AI. The collaboration aims to redefine the future of design, engineering, and manufacturing by combining NVIDIA Omniverse with Dassault's 3DEXPERIENCE platform for real-time simulation and optimization.

### AI-Driven Generative Design Maturity
The generative design landscape continues to mature, with engineers now able to input constraints such as materials, weight, cost, and performance goals, and receive hundreds of optimized design alternatives. However, industry analysis suggests that AI design review tools are currently delivering more measurable ROI than generative design, as the latter still requires significant engineering judgment to evaluate and select from generated options.

### Adam AI Raises Seed Funding
Adam AI, which launched version 1.0 in early 2025, continues to develop its rapid prototyping platform that generates print-ready 3D models from text prompts. The company raised $4.1M in seed funding to expand its text-to-3D capabilities, targeting engineers and designers who need quick physical prototypes without extensive CAD expertise.
