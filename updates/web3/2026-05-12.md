# Web3 Weekly Update — May 12, 2026

## Highlights

- **Senate CLARITY Act Vote Set for May 14**: The U.S. Senate Banking Committee is scheduled to mark up the Digital Asset Market Clarity Act on May 14, with the White House targeting July 4 for final approval — potentially the most significant crypto legislation in U.S. history.
- **$292M Kelp DAO Hack Sets New 2026 Record**: A $292 million exploit of Kelp DAO became the largest DeFi hack of 2026, surpassing the $285 million Drift exploit from April 1.
- **Stablecoin Yield Compromise Reached in Congress**: Lawmakers reportedly reached a deal on whether stablecoin issuers can offer yield or rewards programs, potentially clearing the path for broader market-structure legislation.

## Detailed Updates

### CLARITY Act Heads to Senate Markup
The Digital Asset Market Clarity Act of 2025 is scheduled for markup by the U.S. Senate Banking Committee on May 14, 2026. The bill would establish a clear regulatory framework distinguishing digital commodities from securities, building on the SEC and CFTC's joint classification of Bitcoin, Ether, Solana, and XRP as digital commodities in March 2026. The White House is reportedly targeting July 4 for final approval. Analysts project that passage could drive 30-40% growth in institutional crypto AUM over 12 months. US spot Bitcoin ETFs have already attracted nearly $1 billion in inflows in early May alone.

### Kelp DAO Exploit: $292M Drained
A new record for the largest DeFi hack of 2026 was set when $292 million was drained from Kelp DAO. The exploit surpasses the previous record held by the $285 million Drift exploit on April 1. The attack highlights ongoing security challenges in DeFi protocols despite years of auditing improvements and bug bounty programs.

### Stablecoin Yield Provision Compromise
A major legislative breakthrough occurred when lawmakers reportedly reached a compromise on the contentious stablecoin "yield" provisions. The dispute centered on whether stablecoin issuers should be permitted to offer yield or rewards programs to holders. The compromise could clear the path for broader market-structure legislation and provide regulatory clarity for a sector that has grown to hundreds of billions in market capitalization.

### Chainlink CCIP Crosses $1.3B Weekly Volume
Chainlink's Cross-Chain Interoperability Protocol (CCIP) processed over $1.3 billion in weekly volume, demonstrating growing adoption of cross-chain infrastructure. CCIP enables secure token transfers and messaging between different blockchains, and the volume milestone suggests that multi-chain interoperability is moving from experimental to production-grade infrastructure.

### Polkadot Introduces Hard Supply Cap and JAM Upgrade
Polkadot made significant economic changes by introducing a hard supply cap and reducing token issuance, improving its long-term tokenomics. The upcoming JAM upgrade aims to transform Polkadot from a parachain network into a decentralized compute platform, broadening its use case beyond blockchain interoperability to general-purpose decentralized computation.

### Helium Mobile Crosses 160K Subscribers
Helium's decentralized mobile network passed 160,000 subscribers and is generating actual revenue, making it one of the few Web3 projects with demonstrable real-world product-market fit. The milestone is notable in a space where many projects struggle to show usage beyond speculative trading.

### Japan's Clear Regulatory Framework Attracts Institutional Capital
Japan completed a revised crypto asset regulatory framework in late 2025, creating one of the world's clearest legal environments for DeFi participation. The framework has unlocked institutional capital from major Japanese financial institutions and enabled compliant on-chain yield products at scale, positioning Japan as a leading market for regulated DeFi activity.

### Crypto Market Overview
The total crypto market cap climbed to nearly $2.69 trillion, with Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, and Solana all posting small gains between 1-2%. The new Fed Chair appointment and Base's Azul upgrade are key events to watch in the week ahead, according to CoinDesk's weekly market outlook.
